660nm InGaAlP Chip

WPE Optimization of ams-OSRAM Hyper Red LEDs

A steady trajectory of Wall-Plug Efficiency (WPE) improvement in 660nm Hyper Red LEDs, driven by systematic epitaxial refinement and advanced InGaAlP chip architectures to meet the rigorous demands of greenhouse environments.

2022 | Baseline: OSLON™ Square

WPE: 76.8%

Established as the robust baseline utilizing a ceramic package and thin-film InGaAlP chip design.

2023 | 5th Gen OSLON™ Square

WPE: 78.8% (at 25°C Tj)

A major +2.0 percentage-point leap achieved via optimized InGaAlP epitaxial structure and significantly improved thermal droop behavior.

2024 | OSLON™ Optimal (3030)

Radiant Eff: ~74.3% | Efficacy: ~4.06 µmol/J

Portfolio expansion into compact, cost-optimized 3030 packages. Maintained strong radiant efficiency and 520 mW radiant flux.
